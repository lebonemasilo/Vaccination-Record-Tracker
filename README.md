Vaccination-Record-Tracker
The chosen health scenario is a Vaccination Record Tracker, a system designed to help medical staff efficiently store, update, and manage patient vaccination records. This is especially relevant in Botswana, where clinics (particularly in rural areas like Serowe or smaller health posts) often rely on paper-based systems, making it easy to lose records or delay patient care. By digitizing vaccination data, healthcare workers can quickly access patient histories, ensure timely immunizations, and improve overall public health tracking. The main user actions in the system include adding a new patient and their vaccination details, updating existing records, viewing a patient’s vaccination history, and generating reports or summaries of vaccination coverage. This system helps streamline clinic workflows, reduce errors, and support better healthcare delivery.
